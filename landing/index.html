How FlowTube works

Three moves, no YouTube API.

1

Detect video state

A content script reads YouTube page video state like play, pause, ended, and current tab metadata.

2

Choose the right tab

FlowTube checks open YouTube tabs, keyword signals, manual overrides, and channel preferences.

3

Switch the audio

The extension sends safe play and pause commands, then records diagnostics if Chrome blocks autoplay.

Privacy-first positioning

Your preferences stay in your browser.

FlowTube stores settings, overrides, channel preferences, diagnostics, and undo state with chrome.storage.local. There is no backend, no account requirement, no analytics, and no cloud sync.

Questions people search for

FlowTube FAQ.

Does FlowTube work with YouTube Music?

Yes. FlowTube supports regular YouTube and YouTube Music tabs in Chrome.

Does FlowTube collect my YouTube data?

No. Preferences and diagnostics are stored locally in your browser with Chrome extension storage.

Why did Chrome block autoplay?

Chrome may require one click on the music tab before extensions can start playback automatically.

Is FlowTube affiliated with YouTube or Google?

No. FlowTube is an independent extension and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by YouTube or Google.
